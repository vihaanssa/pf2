Branding

KARIGARI

This work is for a luxury jewelry brand, specializing in
emeralds. The branding includes sleek yet intricate
details with a elegant green color to resonate with the

Typography

FONT DESIGN - JOVIAL

This is a font family that is a display sans-serif typeface with a
geometric and minimalist design. Its clean lines, cuts and
proportional shapes contribute to its modern and versatile aesthetic.

Poster & Packaging Design

PRODUCT POSTERS-

Posters re-imagining and inspired from Rococo & Neo-Classical Art
periods. The aim was to identify color palettes, motifs, visual elements
, iconic design features, design principles, etc. from the movements and
apply them to the respective posters.These posters have a minimal
approach in contract to the grandeur of these movements
